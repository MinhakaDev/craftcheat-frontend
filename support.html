Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install my purchased cheat? ‚ñº

After purchasing, you'll receive download instructions via email. Generally, the installation process involves:

  1. Download the loader from your account dashboard
  2. Disable your antivirus temporarily (our software is clean but may trigger false positives)
  3. Run the loader as administrator
  4. Log in with your CraftCheat credentials
  5. Select the game and features you want to enable
  6. Launch your game through our loader

For game-specific installation guides, please check our knowledge base.

Why is my anti-virus detecting the cheat as malware? ‚ñº

This is a false positive. Game cheats need to modify game memory or intercept DirectX calls which can trigger antivirus detection. Our software is completely safe but uses techniques that antivirus programs typically flag.

To use our products, you'll need to add an exclusion in your antivirus for the loader and cheat files, or temporarily disable your antivirus during use. We guarantee all our products are clean and contain no malicious code.

I got banned. Can I get a refund? ‚ñº

While we strive to make our products as undetectable as possible, we cannot guarantee you will never be banned. Using cheats always carries some risk, which is why we recommend using our HWID spoofer and following all safety guidelines.

As stated in our Terms of Service, we do not offer refunds for bans. However, if you purchased a subscription with ban protection, we'll provide you with a new account license. Please contact support with your order details.

How often are cheats updated? ‚ñº

We update our products regularly to maintain compatibility with game updates and to stay ahead of anti-cheat systems. Major game updates typically receive same-day patches, while minor updates are usually covered within 24-48 hours.

Premium and VIP members receive priority updates. You can check the status of each product on our Status Dashboard in your account area.

How do I configure the aimbot settings? ‚ñº

Our aimbots are highly customizable to suit your playstyle. After launching the cheat, press the designated menu key (default is Insert) to open the configuration panel. From there, you can adjust:

  • Aim smoothness: How naturally the aim moves (higher values look more legit)
  • FOV: The area in which the aimbot will detect enemies
  • Target selection: Priority based on distance, health, etc.
  • Aim key: The button that activates the aimbot
  • Bone selection: Which part of the enemy to target (head, chest, etc.)

We recommend starting with conservative settings and gradually adjusting to avoid detection.
